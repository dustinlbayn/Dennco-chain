# Dennco Capital Strategy

Dennco Chain should be positioned as one technology asset inside the broader Dennco infrastructure platform, not as a speculative coin or stand-alone investment token.

## Core capital position

Dennco is raising company capital to build infrastructure assets, not selling a speculative cryptocurrency.

Recommended positioning:

> Dennco Chain is one technology asset inside Dennco's broader infrastructure platform.

This keeps the capital story centered on Dennco Holding Company and its operating assets, while allowing Dennco Chain to remain a strategic product within the company.

## Company platform structure

```text
Dennco Holding Company
├── Dennco Information Systems
├── The Printing Lounge
├── Dennco Chain
├── cloud hosting
├── communications
├── web-to-print
├── enterprise records
├── digital identity
└── infrastructure services
```

## Fundraising direction

The preferred capital strategy is to raise funds into the company or a dedicated company subsidiary, rather than selling a public investment token.

```text
Investor money → Dennco Holding Company or dedicated subsidiary
Investor receives → equity, SAFE, convertible note, preferred equity, or revenue-share instrument
Company uses funds for → infrastructure, software, staffing, sales, legal, compliance, acquisitions
Dennco Chain → one technology asset inside the company
```

## Investor narrative

Dennco Holding Company is building an integrated technology infrastructure company focused on cloud hosting, communications, publishing infrastructure, enterprise blockchain records, identity systems, and digital business services. Capital raised will support the expansion of Dennco Information Systems, The Printing Lounge, Dennco Chain, and supporting infrastructure.

## What not to do

Dennco Chain should not be promoted as a speculative investment coin.

Avoid positioning such as:

```text
Buy this coin before it goes up.
Invest early in Dennco Coin.
Token holders share in Dennco profits.
Dennco Coin is backed by company growth.
Limited supply guarantees future value.
```

Those statements can create securities, investor-disclosure, money-transmission, tax, and anti-fraud risk.

## Preferred fundraising lanes

### Private company raise

A private raise can be structured around equity, a SAFE, convertible note, preferred equity, or other counsel-approved instrument.

Best suited for:

```text
angel investors
strategic partners
local business owners
private capital
accredited investors
```

### Crowdfunding or public-facing raise

A crowdfunding-style campaign may be considered later through a compliant funding portal and proper disclosures. The raise should still be for company capital, not a speculative token sale.

### Strategic revenue capital

Dennco may also raise non-dilutive revenue through founding customer packages, enterprise memberships, infrastructure credits, managed hosting contracts, API access packages, document verification subscriptions, and vendor onboarding packages.

## Capital package to build

Before approaching investors, Dennco should prepare:

```text
1. Investor pitch deck
2. One-page executive summary
3. Use-of-funds budget
4. Entity structure chart
5. 12-month roadmap
6. 36-month roadmap
7. Current assets and projects list
8. Revenue model
9. Risk disclosure language
10. Counsel-reviewed investment terms
```

## Final positioning statement

> Dennco is raising company capital to build an integrated technology infrastructure platform. Dennco Chain is one strategic asset within that platform, supporting enterprise identity, asset registration, secure business records, document verification, API access, wallet infrastructure, and future digital operating systems.
